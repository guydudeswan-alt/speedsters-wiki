Speedsters

In the Arrowverse, the cosmos is bound by primal cosmic forces. The narrative centers on individuals who tap into these extra-dimensional energy fields, granting them god-like velocity to either protect timelines or modify reality itself.

Barry Allen (The Flash)

Barry Allen is a forensic scientist struck by dark-matter lightning, becoming the generator of the Speed Force.

Deep Lore & Finale Arc

Barry evolved from simple running to phasing through matter and time travel. In the finale, he ends the speedster war peacefully by convincing Cobalt Blue that the positive and negative forces must co-exist to preserve reality, before passing his lightning legacy onto new generations.

Wally West (Kid Flash)

Wally West gained speed via Doctor Alchemy's husk machine, stepping up as Kid Flash to protect Central City.

Deep Lore & Powers

Wally trained with the Legends of Tomorrow, mastering spiritual practices and Zen meditation. This connection allows him to heal rapidly and project energy creatively.

Eobard Thawne (Reverse-Flash)

Eobard Thawne is a brilliant 22nd-century speed rogue and the archenemy of Barry Allen.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Thawne created the Negative Speed Force out of absolute hatred. Plucked from time to fight in the final war, he plans to demolish Barry's legacy but is blasted and neutralized by Allegra Garcia using a massive wave of electromagnetic light energy.

Hunter Zolomon (Zoom)

Hunter Zolomon is a demonic serial killer from Earth-2 hunting down other speedsters to steal their speed vectors.
