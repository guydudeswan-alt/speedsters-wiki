Speedsters

1. Barry Allen (The Flash)

Lore: Generator of the Speed Force. Ending: Convinces Cobalt Blue to co-exist, ending the war peacefully.

2. Wally West (Kid Flash)

Lore: Gained speed via husks. Ending: Masters Zen meditation to manipulate Speed Force energy spiritually.

3. Eobard Thawne (Reverse-Flash)

Lore: Barry's nemesis; created Negative Speed Force. Ending: Neutralized by Allegra Garcia's light blast.

4. Hunter Zolomon (Zoom)

Lore: Earth-2 killer decaying from Velocity-9. Ending: Frozen cells and defeated by nature goddess Khione.

5. Savitar (The God of Speed)

Lore: Corrupted temporal Barry clone in armor. Ending: Core armor panel stabbed and overloaded by Nora (XS).

6. August Heart (Godspeed)

Lore: 2049 scientist using speed drones. Ending: Legions and real form knocked out cold by Cecile's psychic pulse.

7. The Speed Force

Lore: Sentient time energy source. Ending: Continues guiding good speedsters in the physical shape of Nora Allen.

8. The Negative Speed Force

Lore: Dark matrix fueled by rage. Ending: Chooses Eddie Thawne as a peaceful avatar to balance the cosmic cycle.
