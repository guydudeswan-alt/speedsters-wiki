Speedsters

In the Arrowverse, the cosmos is bound by primal cosmic forces. The narrative centers on individuals who tap into these extra-dimensional energy fields, granting them god-like velocity to either protect timelines or modify reality itself.

Barry Allen (The Flash)

Barry Allen is a forensic scientist struck by dark-matter lightning, becoming the generator of the Speed Force.

Deep Lore & Powers

Barry is the literal engine of the positive Speed Force. Over nine seasons, his powers evolved from simple running to phasing through solid matter, throwing lightning, and manipulating time.

Series Finale Arc

In the final episode, Barry faces the ultimate speedster war brought on by Cobalt Blue. Instead of fighting to the death, Barry tracks Eddie into the Negative Speed Force dimension and convinces him that the positive and negative forces must live in a balanced cycle, ending the conflict peacefully. Afterward, Barry releases a massive bolt of lightning into Central City, sharing his powers with Avery Ho, Max Mercury, and Jess Chambers to create a lasting legacy of protection.

Wally West (Kid Flash)

Wally West gained speed via Doctor Alchemy's husk machine, stepping up as Kid Flash to protect Central City.

Deep Lore & Powers

Wally trained with the Legends of Tomorrow, mastering spiritual practices and Zen meditation. This detached him from the mechanical limits of speed, allowing him to project energy creatively.
