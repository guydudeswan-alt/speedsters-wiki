Speedsters

In the Arrowverse, individuals tap into cosmic forces granting god-like velocity to protect timelines or conquer reality, clashing in an epic finale battle.

Barry Allen (The Flash)

⚡ RED

Barry Allen is a forensic scientist struck by dark-matter lightning, becoming the physical engine and soul of the Speed Force.

Deep Lore & Finale Arc

Barry evolved from running to phasing through solid objects and traveling through time. In the series finale, he stops the war peacefully by convincing Cobalt Blue that the positive and negative forces must co-exist to balance the universe, before sharing his speed with future heroes.

Wally West (Kid Flash)

⚡ YEL

Wally West gained speed via Doctor Alchemy's husk machine, joining Team Flash as Kid Flash before venturing out on his own journey.

Deep Lore & Powers

Wally trained alongside the Legends of Tomorrow, mastering advanced Zen meditation and spiritual techniques. This unique approach allows him to heal rapidly, manipulate energy, and interact with the Speed Force plain fluids in a deeply artistic way.

Eobard Thawne (Reverse-Flash)

⚡ NEG

Eobard Thawne is a brilliant rogue scientist from the 22nd century and Barry Allen's absolute historical nemesis.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Thawne created the Negative Speed Force out of pure hatred. Pulucked from time to join the final speedster war, he attempts to smash Barry's legacy forever but is entirely neutralized and vaporized by Allegra Garcia using a massive blast of electromagnetic light energy.

Hunter Zolomon (Zoom)

⚡ BLK

Hunter Zolomon is a terrifying serial killer from Earth-2 obsessed with hunting speedsters to siphon their kinetic energy vectors.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Zoom was originally dragged into the speed dimension and turned into the Black Flash by Time Wraiths. Brought into the final war from 2016, he is permanently stopped by the nature goddess Khione, who freezes his atmospheric moisture to trap his cells entirely.

Savitar (The God of Speed)

⚡ ARM

Savitar is a tragic temporal clone (Time Remnant) of Barry Allen driven insane by abandonment and rejection.

Lore & Definitive Ending

He constructed a giant mechanical metallic armor set and claimed to be the first speedster god. Brought into the final war from 2017, he attacks Barry's daughter, Nora West-Allen (XS), who phases through his arm-blade and stabs his core panel, overloading the armor completely.

August Heart (Godspeed)

⚡ WHT

August Heart is a criminal scientist from 2049 who engineered artificial velocity using specialized tachyon matrix nodes.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Godspeed deploys an army of drone speed clones to harvest organic velocity. In the series finale, his entire clone legion is knocked out simultaneously by Cecile Horton (Virtue) using an amplified telepathic emotion blast across Central City.

The Speed Force

⚡ SF

The Speed Force is a sentient cosmic energy entity representing time progression and movement throughout the multiverse.

Deep Lore

It fuels all positive speed heroes and frequently talks to Barry Allen by manifesting itself in the physical visual form of his deceased mother, Nora Allen, to guide him or protect history's natural order.

The Negative Speed Force

⚡ NSF

The Negative Speed Force is a corrupt, dark energy matrix generated by Thawne that feeds entirely on rage and envy.
