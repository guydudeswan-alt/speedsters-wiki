The Flash

In the Arrowverse, heroic and villainous speedsters tap into cosmic forces granting god-like velocity to either protect the timeline or rewrite reality for absolute power, coming to an explosive final conclusion in the series finale war.

Barry Allen (The Flash)

Barry Allen is a forensic scientist struck by dark-matter lightning, becoming the central generator and engine of the Speed Force.

Deep Lore & Finale Arc

Barry evolved from running to phasing and time travel. In the finale, he ends the speedster war peacefully by convincing Cobalt Blue that the positive and negative forces must co-exist to maintain cosmic balance. He later shares his speed with future heroes Avery Ho, Max Mercury, and Jess Chambers.

Wally West (Kid Flash)

Wally West gained speed via Doctor Alchemy's husk machine, eventually operating alongside the team as Kid Flash.

Deep Lore & Powers

Wally left Central City to train with the Legends of Tomorrow, mastering Zen meditation and spiritual techniques. This unlocked higher-level capabilities, making his speed fluid, adaptive, and deeply tied to cosmic energy projection.

Eobard Thawne (Reverse-Flash)

Eobard Thawne is a brilliant 22nd-century rogue scientist and Barry's absolute historical nemesis.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Thawne created the Negative Speed Force out of pure hatred. Plucked from history for the finale war to target S.T.A.R. Labs, he is permanently stopped by Allegra Garcia, who completely neutralizes him using a massive blast of electromagnetic light energy, dispelling him back to his original timeline erasure point.

Reverse-Flash
Source:Negative Force
Actor:Tom Cavanagh
Fate:Defeated by Allegra

Hunter Zolomon (Zoom)

Hunter Zolomon is a convicted serial killer from Earth-2 obsessed with stealing speed to cure his own cellular decay caused by Velocity-9 drugs.

Lore & Definitive Ending

Originally turned into the Black Flash by Time Wraiths, Zoom is pulled from 2016 into the final timeline war. He attempts to destroy Central City but is intercepted by the nature goddess Khione, who manipulates the atmospheric moisture to instantly freeze his cells and blast him back to his original point in time.
